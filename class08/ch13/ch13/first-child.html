Climate

The Mediterranean has long, hot summers and mild, wet winters. Most of the rain falls during the winter months.

Getting There

All countries on the northern side of the Mediterranean are well served by airports and rail services. There are also frequent ferries between the mainland and islands. The Mediterranean is also popular with cruise ships.
